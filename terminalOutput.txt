As a Senior Server Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining the server-side infrastructure and services for our Unity-based game. The ideal candidate has a strong background in server-side development, a deep understanding of networking protocols, and experience in Unity game development.</p>

<li>Debug and troubleshoot server-related issues, identify performance bottlenecks, and implement necessary optimizations.</li>

<li>Strong proficiency in server-side programming languages such as Node.js.</li>
<li>Extensive experience with backend technologies and frameworks such as Node.js, postgres and redis.</li>
<li>Deep understanding of networking protocols, client-server architectures, and server-authoritative game logic.</li>

<li>Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud, and knowledge of serverless architecture.</li>
<li>Familiarity with SQL and NoSQL databases, such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, or Redis.</li>
<li>Solid understanding of software development principles, version control systems, and agile methodologies.</li>
